6.046 algorithms design, hw

Brief guide: be clear and precise.

Goal : To communicate.

Form: a short essay. The body of the essay provides:

  1. Description of algorithm. Pseudocode
  2. worked example/diagram
  3. Proof of the correctness
  4. annalysis of runtime
